# MONEY PRINTER PRO – Institutional Chart Analysis Workflow

## Step 1: Chart Setup (Top-Down Analysis)

### Set Symbol & Core Timeframes

1. `chart_set_symbol`
2. Analyze across multiple timeframes:

**HTF Bias**

* Weekly (macro trend)
* Daily (directional bias)

**Execution Timeframes**

* 4H → major structure
* 1H → internal structure
* 15m / 5m → entry trigger

3. `chart_set_timeframe`

Required order:

Weekly → Daily → 4H → 1H → 15m → 5m

Goal:

* Identify alignment or conflict between timeframes.

---

## Step 2: Market Structure & Smart Money Concepts (SMC)

### Analyze Structure

Mark:

* External structure
* Internal structure
* Swing highs/lows
* Higher highs / lower lows

Look for:

* BOS (Break of Structure)
* MSS / ChoCH (Market Structure Shift)
* Liquidity sweeps
* Equal highs / equal lows
* Inducement
* Stop hunts

### Mark Institutional Zones

Draw:

* Demand zones
* Supply zones
* Order Blocks
* Breaker Blocks
* Mitigation Blocks
* Fair Value Gaps (FVG)

Questions:

* Is price in premium or discount?
* Has liquidity already been taken?
* Is price seeking external liquidity?

---

## Step 3: Volume + Volume Price Action (VPAC/VSA)

### Add Indicators

Use `chart_manage_indicator`:

Required:

* "Volume"
* "VWAP"

Optional:

* "Moving Average Exponential" (20/50/200)
* "Average True Range"

Interpretation:

#### Bullish Signs

* Expansion candle + high volume
* Pullback on weak volume
* Absorption at demand
* Reclaim with participation

#### Bearish Signs

* Climax volume rejection
* Rising price + falling volume
* Distribution near highs
* Failed breakout

Check:

* Effort vs result
* Climactic exhaustion
* Volume confirmation
* Low volume fake breakouts

## Step 5: Price Action + Trading Theories

Evaluate:

### Wyckoff Logic

* Accumulation
* Distribution
* Spring
* Upthrust

### Auction Theory

* Acceptance
* Rejection
* Range expansion

### Liquidity Theory

* Buy-side liquidity
* Sell-side liquidity
* Engineered moves

### Candle Behavior

* Displacement candles
* Compression
* Wicks/rejections
* Break & retest quality

Avoid:

* Chasing impulse candles
* Mid-range entries
* Low liquidity hours
